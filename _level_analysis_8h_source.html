27 /**
28  * @class RamLevelAnalysis
29  * @brief A Ram Analysis for determining the level of a expression/condition
30  *
31  * The expression is determined by the TupleElement of an expression/condition
32  * with the highest tuple-id number. Note in the implementation we assume that the
33  * tuple-id of RamTupleOperation operations are ordered, i.e., the most-outer loop has the
34  * smallest tuple-id and the most inner-loop has the largest tuple-id number.
35  *
36  * If an expression/condition does not contain an TupleElement accessing an element
37  * of a tuple, the analysis yields -1 for denoting that the expression/condition
38  * can be executed outside of the loop-nest, i.e., the expression/condition is
39  * independent of data stemming from relations.
40  *
41  */
